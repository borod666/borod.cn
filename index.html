About Borod

成都博睿模具有限公司是一家专业从事高精密塑料模具设计、开发与制造的现代化企业。深耕行业二十余年，我们专注于为客户提供从产品评估、模具设计到批量生产的一站式精密制造解决方案。

Chengdu Borui Mould Co., Ltd. is a modern enterprise specializing in high-precision plastic mould design, development and manufacturing. With over 20 years of industry experience, we focus on providing one-stop precision manufacturing solutions from product evaluation and mould design to mass production.


产品覆盖汽车零部件、电子电器、医疗器械、日用消费品等多个领域。我们始终坚持「精度优先、客户至上」的理念，以可靠的产品质量和完善的售后服务赢得市场信赖。

Our products cover automotive parts, electronics, medical devices, consumer goods and more. We always adhere to the concept of "Precision First, Customer First" and have earned market trust with reliable product quality and comprehensive after-sales service.
